I have recently joined the Secrier Lab to conduct my PhD in cancer genomics, funded by the MRC Doctoral Training Partnership programme at UCL. I am researching mutational signatures associated with deficiencies in the DNA damage depair pathways, and their role in driving cancer development.

I graduated from UCL in 2019 with an MSci in Natural Sciences. I conducted my Masters project under Dr Garrett Hellenthal here at the UCL Genetics Institute, where I was working on identifying genetic predisposition to variable methylation patterns.
